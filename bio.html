Historical info:

I was born and raised in Long Beach, CA. I have a brother and a sister. I met my best friends in Kindergarten and though friendships between us have ebbed and flowed over the years, we are as close as ever as adults.

I went to college at Tulane University in New Orleans, LA. I started at University of San Francisco but decided to reassess my goals after the first year. I took a semester to think and also took some classes locally, at CSULB and LB city college, before deciding to move to New Orleans and attend Tulane.

After college I moved back to California and worked for my family's small business. I tried out a few things over the years. Took classes in Early Childhood Development, Child Life Science, and Anatomy. I was in search of something new to learn and a new career path. Then I found coding.

Along the way, I met my husband. He introduced me to rock climbing and though the pandemic halted that practice a bit, we did build a climbing wall for our backyard.

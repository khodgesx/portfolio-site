My name is Kelly Hodges and I am currently working through the Software Engineering Immersive Program at General Assembly.

I love to learn and believe it is healthy to keep learning and challenging yourself throughout your life. There is a lot of learning to be had when it comes to software engineering, I look forward to my journey in this new universe.

I have an extreme love for baking and chomping pizza. I have a library of photos of the creative pizzas that my husband and I make, scroll down to see a few.

I studied neuroscience at Tulane University and hold a special place in my heart for all things science and of course, New Orleans.

I have a very sweet senior pup named Midge. She gives me a quick bark to let me know I've been at the computer too long or when she is requesting an early dinner.

I love to be active whether that is a quick walk with said dog, working out at home, riding my bike, or rock climbing. I am also training for a bike-packing trip in Europe in May 2022 with my husband and some friends.

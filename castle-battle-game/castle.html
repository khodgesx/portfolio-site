Castle Battle

How to play Castle Battle

Castle Battle is you versus the computer!

You each have barracks that you will fill with peons that either attack your opponent or repair your barracks after attacks from your opponent.

For each turn, you get a choice to either create a peon or to select a peon.

If you choose to create a peon, you create it and give it a name and it assumes the position in your barracks but it will not have a job yet!

If you choose to select a peon, you select it by name and give it a job type: attack or repair.

For each repair-peon in your barracks, your barracks will increase hp by 1 point and for each attack-peon, it will attack your opponent and decrease their health by 1 point!

After you've either created or selected a peon (you only get to do one!) the computer will take its turn...

The computer will choose to either attack you or repair its barracks
